Private because nothing leaves.

Most AI assistants ship every email you receive to someone else's server. Offline Email AI was built to do the opposite.

No developer backend. The app has no server to send your data to — because there is no server.

No analytics, no trackers. Not in the main app, not in a framework, not anywhere.

Credentials in the Keychain. Your email password never touches a plaintext file or a network log.

Direct to your provider. IMAP and SMTP traffic goes straight to your mail server over TLS.

Cloud is opt-in. You can plug in an OpenAI, Anthropic, or Gemini key if you prefer — but it's off by default.

Works with the mail you already have.

Gmail, iCloud, Outlook, Yahoo, Fastmail, or your own IMAP server. No forwarding, no import, no new inbox to learn.

Built for Apple silicon

Metal acceleration on A17 and newer. A low-memory path keeps iPhone 12-class devices smooth.

No subscription

Pay once on the App Store. No ongoing charges, no in-app purchases hiding features.

Open-source core

Built on llama.cpp and Llama 3.2. The engine is auditable; the app is not spying on you.
